Australia in the World

According to WHO (2020, para. 10), “The importance of facts not fear in the new coronavirus outbreak. People must have access to accurate information to protect themselves and others”. At the same time, the number of Covid-19 cases in Australia has surged, which has attracted the attention of many international students and the world. This visualization mainly focuses on the development of the Covid-19 in Australia, and comprehensively analyses the trend and regional distribution of the Covid-19 in recent months. This allows users to obtain reliable information more effectively.

Gender and Age Effects

"If you look at the data across the world, there are as many men as women that are infected. It’s just the severity of disease that is stronger in most populations in men" (Mauvais-Jarvis, as cited in Guarino, 2020, para. 7). Generally speaking, according to the donut chart on the right, the number of deaths and the number of infections is positively correlated, the confirmed rate of men will be higher, and the death rate of women will be lower.

Females over 86 years old have the highest probability of being infected, while males between 76 and 85 years old have the highest possibility of being infected. People under 15 years of age are less likely to be infected, only about 0.18% to 0.32%. In the two age groups 36-45 and 56-65, there is little difference in the likelihood of males and females being susceptible to infection. From the overall trend, the increase in age will greatly affect the probability of infection.

Riskiest Activities

The graph on the right shows some activities of spreading covid-19. The size of the text changes according to the number of occurrences of each activity. The main activity for covid-19 is to hug or shake hands. According to WHO (2020, para. 1), “Hands have a crucial role in the transmission of COVID-19”. Therefore, wash your hands frequently, the correct way to wash your hands is the main way to prevent the spread of covid-19. Not only that, some crowded occasions, such as sports fields, beaches, and supermarkets, are the main activities for the spread of covid-19.
